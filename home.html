About Us

NITS Eco Club is an officially recognised club under the Gymkhana Union Body, NIT Silchar. The principal objective of this club is to keep the campus clean, and preseve the diverse flora and fauna our institution has to offer. Under this club, NIT Silchar has achieved greater heights. It has been placed 200th among 956 universites worldwide, and 5th among universities in India by the UI Green Metric World University Rankings, 2021, which is a matter of great pride for the institution.
